## Heading
Sustainability — "Caring for all of our resources."

## Body
Commitment to producing quality products that create "economic, environmental and social benefits for our employees, our customers and the communities we serve," aligned with family legacy values.

## Three pillars
1. **Environmental Footprint** — soil quality enhancement, land use improvement, reductions in greenhouse gas emissions, waste, energy, and water consumption
2. **Social Responsibility** — respect, equal opportunity, health/safety, competitive benefits, flexible scheduling, professional development, community engagement (charitable giving, volunteerism)
3. **Economic Stability** — profitability alongside customer value and societal benefit; governance, compliance, risk management
