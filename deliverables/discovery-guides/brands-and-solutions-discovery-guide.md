## Discussion guide — Brands page

1. **Inclusion / exclusion**
   - Walk every brand on the current list — still active, still worth featuring?
   - Anything missing that should be added?
   - Does any brand in the room want to opt out of being listed publicly? (Flagged by Johnnie as a real possibility — surface it now, not after wireframes.)
   - Any nested/sub-brand relationships the flat list above doesn't capture (e.g., Frostline vs. Frosty Boy)?
2. **Grouping & hierarchy**
   - Confirm the four groupings (Kent Nutrition, GPC, Pet Care, Food & Hydration) still match how the business thinks about itself.
   - Any brand that arguably spans more than one group?
3. **Per-brand content**
   - One-sentence description each brand wants displayed on the page.
   - Who owns/can provide final logo assets for each brand?
   - Does each brand want an outbound link to its own site, or stay landing-page-only within kentww.com?
4. **Page framing**
   - What's the one- or two-sentence narrative for "why one parent company has this many brands"?
   - Any preference on presentation (filterable grid, grouped list, something else)?

## Discussion guide — Solutions page

1. **Naming**
   - Confirm "Solutions" resonates with the full cohort, not just Johnnie/Leandra who picked it.
2. **Pillars**
   - Does the current four-pillar structure (Plant-Based Products, Future of Agriculture, Scientific Achievement, Innovative Packaging) still reflect the business, or has the story shifted since this copy was written?
   - Any new proof points — recent launches, awards, patents — to fold in?
3. **Storytelling**
   - Of the current featured stories (ARGIS® award, probiotic launch, Frostline flavor award), which are must-keep vs. retireable?
   - Anything in the pipeline (even unannounced) the page should tease?
4. **Tone & cross-linking**
   - Balance between "innovation for the product/business" and "innovation for people/community" framing.
   - Should this page cross-link into Brands and/or Sustainability, and if so, how explicitly?
